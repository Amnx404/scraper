We first highlight how Gaussian Process (GP) regression can be leveraged to capture complex vehicle dynamics and unmodeled effects, thereby improving the fidelity of prediction models in the Model Predictive Control (MPC) framework to improve the trajectory tracking performance. Building on this foundation, we introduce game-theoretic decision-making methods that enable autonomous vehicles to anticipate and respond to the diverse behaviors of heterogeneous traffic participants, facilitating safe and socially aware interaction.
