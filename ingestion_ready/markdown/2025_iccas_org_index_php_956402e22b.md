### How to Submit Your Paper<\/h3>\n

All Papers, and organized session proposals must be submitted electronically through the\u00a0 _[PaperCept Conference Management System<\/a><\/u>.
\nAuthors are advised to read\u00a0[ _PaperCept\u2019s Getting Started Manual <\/u><\/a>\u00a0for Authors and\u00a0 _[PDF Compliance Guidelines<\/a><\/u>\u00a0carefully.<\/p>\n>**_[Go to PaperPlaza Conference Review Management System<\/a><\/u><\/strong> and find ICCAS 2025<\/p>\n[![\\"\\"](\\"http:\\/\\/2025.iccas.org\\/wp-content\\/uploads\\/2025\\/03\\/\\ud648\\ud398\\uc774\\uc9c0.png\\")<\/a><\/p>\n\u2663Paper Template of ICCAS 2025\u00a0<\/span><\/h5>\n[![\\"\\"](\\"http:\\/\\/2025.iccas.org\\/wp-content\\/uploads\\/2025\\/05\\/\\uadf8\\ub9bc1.jpg\\")<\/a>\u00a0 \u00a0 \u00a0 \u00a0 [![\\"\\"](\\"http:\\/\\/2025.iccas.org\\/wp-content\\/uploads\\/2025\\/05\\/\\uadf8\\ub9bc2.jpg\\")<\/a><\/div>\n<\/div>\n\n\u2663Submission Type<\/span><\/h5>\n

\n
  * Regular Paper (3~6 pages)<\/li>\n<\/ul>\nFull papers must be submitted for review. Authors can choose whether the paper will be indexed in IEEE Xplore, SCOPUS, and EI compendex or not. Accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore subject to meeting IEEE Xplore\u2019s scope and quality requirements.<\/p>\n\n
    * Organized Session (1~6 pages)<\/li>\n<\/ul>\nOrganized Session will be organized to present work that surrounds unified themes and the organizer can set up the presentation time. Authors who accept invitations from organizers may submit their work as \u201cOrganized Session Paper\u201d.<\/p>\n\n
      * Interactive Poster Paper (1~2 pages)<\/li>\n<\/ul>\nInteractive Poster Papers must be submitted for review.\u00a0 Interactive Poster Paper is aimed to present the result of your research in more Interactive way with audiences.<\/p>\n\n
        * Dissemination Paper<\/li>\n<\/ul>\nDissemination contributions provide opportunities to authors whose papers have been recently accepted by journals to showcase their results to a broader audience at the ICCAS 2025.\u00a0 The titles and abstracts will be included in the conference program, but they will NOT be included in proceedings.<\/p>\n

\nOPTION: ICCAS with IJCAS Option<\/h3>\nThe International Conference on Control, Automation, and Systems (ICCAS) aims to bring together researchers and engineers in the fields of control, automation, robotics, and systems engineering. The scope of the conference is well aligned with the scope of the International Journal of Control, Automation, and Systems (IJCAS).<\/p>\nThe purpose of the ICCAS with IJCAS option is to provide a possibility to publish a paper in the Journal, but present it at ICCAS.\u00a0**The authors can submit an extended abstract (1 or 2 pages) to ICCAS, and simultaneously submit a journal version (at least 6 pages) to IJCAS. <\/strong>\u00a0Note that the submission deadline for such joint contributions is May 31, 2025.<\/p>\nAuthors of conference papers submitted at ICCAS 2025 ([https:\/\/2025.iccas.org<\/a>) are encouraged to submit extended versions (at least 6 pages) to the Special Issue of ICCAS 2025 of the International Journal of Control, Automation, and Systems (IJCAS). The manuscript submitted to the special issue should be an extended version from the conference version.<\/p>\nSubmitted manuscripts to the special issue should neither have been published nor be under review elsewhere. They will undergo peer-review following the IJCAS author guidelines. Accepted papers are tentatively scheduled for publication in the\u00a0 _2025 issue of IJCAS <\/u>.<\/p>\n**What is the ICCAS with IJCAS option? <\/strong><\/p>\nA manuscript submitted to ICCAS with IJCAS option may be accepted both by ICCAS and IJCAS, by one of them, or by neither. If the paper is accepted only by IJCAS it will appear on Web of Science as a journal paper. If it is accepted only by ICCAS, it will be considered as a normal ICCAS paper.<\/p>\n**How to submit the papers to ICCAS and IJCAS? <\/strong><\/p>\nThe authors should submit an extended abstract (1 or 2 pages) to ICCAS, and simultaneously submit a journal version (at least 6 pages) to IJCAS.<\/p>\n1) At ICCAS\u00a0([https:\/\/2025.iccas.org<\/a>), please check the \u201cYes\u201d option as:<\/p>\nI like to submit the extended version of this paper to IJCAS.\u00a0\u00a0** _Yes <\/u><\/strong>\u00a0 No
\n(If IJCAS accepts your extended paper, the conference paper will not
\nbe indexed in IEEE Xplore. You should also present the paper at ICCAS 2025.)<\/p>\n2) At IJCAS ([https:\/\/ijcas.org<\/a>), the authors should select the \u201cSpecial Issue: ICCAS 2025\u201d category.<\/p>\n3) After completing the submission to ICCAS and IJCAS, please inform us of your ICCAS paper number to IJCAS Secretariat at\u00a0[journal@ijcas.com<\/a>.<\/p>\n<\/div>\n**How to submit the final papers to ICCAS? <\/strong><\/p>\nWhen submitting a paper under the ICCAS + IJCAS option, you must upload the ICCAS final paper (for short papers, a 2~3 page extended abstract) by the ICCAS deadline (August 31). If your IJCAS manuscript is ultimately accepted, the finalized ICCAS paper will appear only in the ICCAS proceedings and will not be posted on IEEE Xplore, regardless of whether you initially selected that option. If the IJCAS manuscript is rejected, however, the finalized ICAS paper will be published in the ICCAS proceedings, and inclusion in IEEE Xplore will then depend on whether you chose to have your ICCAS paper posted there.<\/p>\n\n** _Important Dates for IJCAS <\/u><\/strong>**_
