**The 25th International Conference on Control, Automation, and Systems (ICCAS 2025)** will be held on November 4 – 7, 2025 at Incheon, Korea. The aim of ICCAS 2025 is to bring together professors, researchers, engineers, and students from worldwide to present their recent works and discuss the state-of-the-art technologies related to control, automation, robotics and systems.
