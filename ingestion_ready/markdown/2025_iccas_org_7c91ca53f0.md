### How to Submit Your Paper

All Papers, and organized session proposals must be submitted electronically through the  _[PaperCept Conference Management System](https://ras.papercept.net/conferences/scripts/start.pl)_.
Authors are advised to read [ _PaperCept’s Getting Started Manual_](https://2024.iccas.org/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/Submission-Guideline.pdf) for Authors and  _[PDF Compliance Guidelines](https://ras.papercept.net/conferences/support/support.php)_ carefully.
