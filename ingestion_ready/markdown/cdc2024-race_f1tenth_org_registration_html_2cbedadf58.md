##  Registration

This competition is open for everyone of all levels, everyone is welcome to participate in this competition. A team can consist of multiple teammates. Teams with only one person are also allowed. Teams that take part in the in-person competition need to provide and build an F1TENTH car by themselves. To register in the 2nd F1tenth Sim Racing League, please refer to the [Sim Racing Registration page](https://autodrive-ecosystem.github.io/competitions/f1tenth-sim-racing-cdc-2024/#registration).
The following Google form is only for preliminary registration and for orientation and information sessions. Registration to CDC 2024 is expected for all competitors.

[Registration Open](https://forms.gle/deacCAPfDxbLyTFa6)
