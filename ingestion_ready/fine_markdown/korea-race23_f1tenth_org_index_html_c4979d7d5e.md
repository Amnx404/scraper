# The 2nd F1Tenth Korea Championship

**Event:** The International Conference on Control, Automation, and Systems (ICCAS) 2023
**Dates:** October 17th - October 19th, 2023
**Location:** SonoCalm Yeosu, Yeosu, Korea [Map](https://goo.gl/maps/xxcF9gofUYW8PUyT9)

## F1TENTH KOREA CHAMPIONSHIP

F1TENTH Autonomous Racing is a semi-regular competition organized by an international community of researchers, engineers, and autonomous systems enthusiasts.

The 2nd F1Tenth Korea Championship is an in-person competition. Teams will build a 1:10 scaled autonomous race car according to a given specification and write software for it to minimize lap time without crashing.

**In-person competition details:** Participants will travel to Yeosu-si, Jeollanam-do, Republic of Korea, bringing their own F1TENTH cars and software. Organizers will provide the race setup, track, infrastructure, and manage the race.
