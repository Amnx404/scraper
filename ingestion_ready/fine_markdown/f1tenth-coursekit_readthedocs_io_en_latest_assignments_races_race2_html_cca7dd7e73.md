# Race 2

**Track:** Choose a closed-loop track. At Penn, students race around the hallways in the Levine building. The track can be the same as Race 1 or different.

The hallways on the second floor of the Levine building form a rectangular track.

**Race Prep:** Teams should be given the track beforehand to map it.

**Race Day:** The format is similar to [Race 1](race1.html#doc-race1). View the [Race2 Rubric](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1gvkIyaZuPUC6-Cb7FpFiT8ZrpwOfUKHX/edit?usp=sharing&ouid=116660630717436430566&rtpof=true&sd=true) and [Grading](../grading.html#doc-grading) to see how teams are evaluated. If time permits, a practice session can be held beforehand. Otherwise, have teams run their vehicle around the track and record each lap time and number of consecutive laps. There are two heats, giving teams two chances to complete the race.
