# 20TH F1TENTH AUTONOMOUS GRAND PRIX

27th IEEE International Conference on Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITSC 2024)
September 24th - 27th, 2024
Edmonton Convention Centre, Edmonton, Canada

## 20TH F1TENTH AUTONOMOUS GRAND PRIX

F1TENTH Autonomous Racing is a competition organized by an international community of researchers, engineers, and autonomous systems enthusiasts. Teams participating in the 20th F1TENTH Grand Prix at ITSC 2024 will build a 1:10 scaled autonomous race car according to a given specification and write software for it to achieve the competition objectives: Don't crash and minimize lap time.

ITSC 2024 will host an in-person competition. The main focus is on physical cars. Each team will bring their own F1TENTH car and develop its software. Teams have access to a detailed build manual and can use open-source software to get started. The organizers provide the race setup (rules, submissions, guidelines), the track, and related infrastructure, and organize the race itself.

For the competition, teams first participate in a qualification session to determine seeding. This session involves a time trial where teams must demonstrate they can finish laps without colliding with track boundaries. Afterwards, teams compete in a knockout phase in head-to-head races. Since vehicles and hardware are standardized, teams must develop robust perception, planning, and control algorithms that can handle the uncertainties of a new track and new competitors. The Grand Prix phase pits competitors against each other on the same track.
