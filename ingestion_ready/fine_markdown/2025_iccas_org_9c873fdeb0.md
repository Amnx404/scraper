# The 3rd Koh Young AI Competition 2025

**Date:** September 15 ~ October 10, 2025
**Website:** <HTTP://KohYoungAi.iccas.org>
**Organized by:** Institute of Control Robotics and Systems (ICROS)
**Sponsored by:** Koh Young Technology

##### **Purpose**

Artificial intelligence (AI) is increasingly applied to improve operational efficiency in various industrial fields. In line with this trend, ICROS and Koh Young jointly host the AI Challenge to encourage students to compete in solving an efficient decision-making problem within the manufacturing inspection process.

##### **Participants**

Graduate and undergraduate students interested in AI development for industrial data are eligible. The maximum team size is 5 members.

##### **Registration**

1.  Download and fill out the registration form: [_Click & Download the form_](https://2025.iccas.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/3rd-Koh-Young-AI-Competition_Entry_Form.docx)
2.  Submit the completed form to [ _conference@icros.org_](mailto:conference@icros.org) and [ _sooheehan@postech.ac.kr_](mailto:sooheehan@postech.ac.kr).

##### **Main Schedule**

*   Registration opening: August 11 – September 12, 2025
*   Competition: September 15 – October 10, 2025
*   Award ceremony: November 6, 2025

All winners must register for ICCAS 2025. Winners will have the opportunity to present their algorithms in a special session and are required to open-source their solutions. The oral presentations of the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place winners will be recorded and uploaded to YouTube.

*Note: The competition schedule is subject to minor changes.*

##### **Prizes**

*   1st place: 5,000,000 KRW
*   2nd place: 3,000,000 KRW
*   3rd place: 1,000,000 KRW
