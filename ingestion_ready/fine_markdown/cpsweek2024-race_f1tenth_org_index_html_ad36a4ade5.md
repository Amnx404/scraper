## 17th F1TENTH Autonomous Grand Prix

[Registration Open](https://forms.gle/rSe3zCJRNALHn2j18)

F1TENTH Autonomous Racing is a competition organized by researchers, engineers, and autonomous systems enthusiasts. Teams participating in the 17th F1TENTH Grand Prix at CPS-IoT Week 2024 will build a 1:10 scaled autonomous race car and develop software to minimize lap times without crashing.

![](images/F1TENTH/f1tenth_video.gif)

CPS-IoT Week 2024 will host an in-person competition with physical cars. Each team will bring their own F1TENTH car and software. Teams have access to a build manual and open-source software. Organizers provide the race setup, track, infrastructure, and manage the race.

Competitions begin with a qualification round to determine seeding. Teams must demonstrate they can complete a time trial on a map without obstacles and finish laps without collision on the same map with unknown obstacles. Afterward, teams compete in a knockout phase with head-to-head racing. Since vehicles and hardware are standardized, teams must develop robust perception, planning, and control algorithms to handle uncertainties of new tracks and competitors.
