## Registration

This competition is open to everyone of all levels. Teams can consist of multiple teammates or just one person. Teams participating in the in-person competition must provide and build their own Roboracer car.

For registration in the 6th Roboracer Sim Racing League, please refer to the [Sim Racing Registration page](https://autodrive-ecosystem.github.io/competitions/roboracer-sim-racing-iv-2026/#registration). The provided Google form is for preliminary registration and for orientation and information sessions only. Registration for the IV 2026 event is required for all competitors.

[Registration Open](https://forms.gle/Dgcv3Ta1Aq354GtA7)
