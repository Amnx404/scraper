This award recognizes outstanding papers first authored by a student, based on technical contribution and presentation quality. All award winners will be announced at the Banquet.

**Eligibility:** The applicant must be a student and the first author. The student author must also present the paper at the conference. A student is defined as a full-time student enrolled for a degree or diploma at a university or similar institution.

**Application:** Authors can apply for this award by selecting the Student Best Paper award option during initial submission.

**Evaluation:** Award Committee members will review candidate papers again after the regular review process. The Award Committee will also examine the presentation during the conference. All Best Student Paper Award candidates will be assigned to an oral session for presentation.
