We are providing the following information regarding inquiries from domestic authors about the BK21 standards for the ICCAS 2025 International Conference.

(Please note that this is an international conference according to BK21 standards.)

### ICCAS 2025 Final Paper Submission Statistics

*   **Total Submissions:** 483 papers from 31 countries
*   **Oral Presentation Papers:** 317 papers (175 domestic, 142 international)
*   **Proportion of International Papers among Oral Presentations:** Approximately 44.79% (satisfies the requirement of over one-third)

### Standards for International Conferences Held in Korea

1.  **Participating Countries:** 4 or more
2.  **Oral Presentations:** 20 or more
3.  **International Presenters:** One-third or more of oral presentation presenters must be foreign nationals.
