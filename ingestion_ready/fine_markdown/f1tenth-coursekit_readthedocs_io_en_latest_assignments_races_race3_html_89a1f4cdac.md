# Race 3

**Track:**
*   Choose a new track that is different from Race 1 and Race 2.
*   The track should be large and wide enough to accommodate two vehicles racing head-to-head.
*   Use flexible air conditioner exhaust tubes to set up the track. These are soft enough that crashes will not damage the vehicle.
*   Laser cut holders to secure the track in place, as students will be mapping it and the track should not change.

**Race Prep:**
*   Teams should be given the track beforehand to map it on their own time.

**Race Day:**
*   Refer to the [Race3 Rubric](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1gvkIyaZuPUC6-Cb7FpFiT8ZrpwOfUKHX/edit?usp=sharing&ouid=116660630717436430566&rtpof=true&sd=true) and [Grading](../grading.html#doc-grading) for evaluation criteria.
*   Depending on allocated time, a practice session can be held beforehand.
*   Teams can then complete one or two heats of timed trials (similar to Race 1 and Race 2) to seed the final race.
*   Alternatively, timed trials for seeding can be conducted the day before.
