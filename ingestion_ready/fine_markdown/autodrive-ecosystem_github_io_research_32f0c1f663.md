# RESEARCH

This page hosts research outcomes that employ or contribute to the AutoDRIVE Ecosystem in the form of publications, reports, and theses. We encourage you to read and cite the relevant research artifacts if you use any part of this project for your research.

**Tip:** Do you use the AutoDRIVE Ecosystem for your research? Please contact [Chinmay Samak](mailto:csamak@clemson.edu) or [Tanmay Samak](mailto:tsamak@clemson.edu) if you would like to feature your research on AutoDRIVE's website.
