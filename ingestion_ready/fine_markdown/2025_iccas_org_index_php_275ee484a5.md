# Oral Session Guideline

### How to present your paper?

*   **Time of Presentation:** 15 minutes
    The presenters should ensure their presentation fits within the assigned time slot, leaving at least 3 minutes for questions. The official language of the presentation is English.
*   You will present your material directly in the conference room.

### Presentation Materials

1.  Prepare your presentation file in Microsoft PowerPoint or Adobe PDF format in English. Bring a USB memory stick containing your presentation file.
2.  It is recommended that presenters save their material to the furnished notebook running Microsoft Windows before the session starts. Using your own laptop or smart device is possible if it has an HDMI output, but it is not recommended.
3.  If your presentation includes animations or short movies, check the technical aspects beforehand.

### Audio-Visual Equipment

1.  Each session room will be equipped with a Beam Projector and a Laptop for all presentations. The computers will have Microsoft PowerPoint and Adobe Acrobat Reader installed. To avoid technical issues, presenters are strongly encouraged to use the provided equipment.
2.  Using your laptop computer or other smart devices is possible if they have an HDMI output, but it is not recommended. If you must use your own laptop, bring all necessary adaptors compatible with the beam projector. You must also inform the session room staff that you will be using your own equipment.
