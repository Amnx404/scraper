## Registration

This competition is open to everyone of all levels. Teams can consist of multiple teammates or just one person. Teams participating in the in-person competition must provide and build their own Roboracer car. The Google form is for preliminary registration and for orientation and information sessions. Registration for CDC 2025 is required for all competitors.
