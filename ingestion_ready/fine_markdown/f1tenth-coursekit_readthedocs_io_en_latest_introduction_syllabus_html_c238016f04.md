# Syllabus

This is a semester-long course, but it can be adapted for a quarter system or shorter duration by selecting specific lectures and topics.

The course comprises four main components:

1.  **Lectures:** Conducted in a "clab" (classroom + lab) style, with two 80-minute sessions per week. Each session includes 60 minutes of lecture and 20 minutes of tutorial/lab work. Some lectures may be shorter than others. If taught in a traditional lecture and lab format, this would equate to 2 hours of lectures per week plus a 3-hour lab.

2.  **Labs:** Completed outside of class time, allowing students to apply concepts learned in lectures.

3.  **Races:** Three races are scheduled, each designed to assess students' application of different course components. See [Races Assignments](../assignments/races/index.html#doc-races).

4.  **Final Project:** Students propose and solve a problem using the RoboRacer Autonomous Vehicle System.

Students will work in teams of 3-4, ideally with members possessing diverse skills. Team formation takes place during [Lecture 5](../lectures/ModuleB/lecture05.html#doc-lecture05).
