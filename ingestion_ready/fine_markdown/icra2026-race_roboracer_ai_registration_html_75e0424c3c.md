## Registration

This competition is open to everyone of all levels. Teams can consist of multiple teammates or just one person. Teams participating in the in-person competition must provide and build their own Roboracer car.

To register for the 5th Roboracer Sim Racing League, please visit the [Sim Racing Registration page](https://autodrive-ecosystem.github.io/competitions/roboracer-sim-racing-icra-2026/#registration).

[Register Your Team](https://selapp.lanthan.at/?selection=icra2026)

For any questions regarding participation, contact us at [roboracer2026icra@tuwien.ac.at](mailto:roboracer2026icra@tuwien.ac.at).

### Accommodation

Participants must organize and pay for their own accommodation. The following options are suggested, but the organizers are not affiliated with them and receive no benefits.

*   **Vienna Brigittenau - Youth Hostel**
    *   Webpage: <https://www.oejhv.at/en/youth-hostels/vienna/1200-vienna/>
    *   Address: Adalbert Stifter Straße 73, A-1200 Wien, AUSTRIA
    *   Public transport: Direct bus connection (line 11A) to the conference location (approx. 25 minutes).
    *   Breakfast is included.
    *   Booking is per team directly with the hostel. Check the webpage for room rates.
*   **Official ICRA Conference Hotels**
    *   Webpage: <https://2026.ieee-icra.org/attend/housing-information/>
    *   Consider these hotels based on your budget.
