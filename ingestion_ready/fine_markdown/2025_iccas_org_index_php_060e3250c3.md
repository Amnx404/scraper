# Poster Session Guideline

### How to prepare your poster?

**For presenters:** Please prepare your poster in A0 (84 cm X 118 cm) in portrait format as follows:

1.  The display panel size is about 1m wide and 2m high. The panel will be arranged in the lobby on the 3rd floor.
2.  Supplies (glue, tape, and others) for mounting will be provided during the poster sessions.
3.  The poster is recommended to be in the A0 (84 cm X 118 cm) portrait format. Use an appropriate font size for the posters so that they are readable by attendees. The poster must be printed beforehand and brought along to the conference by the presenter.
4.  Authors should stand by their poster panels to answer questions during the entire presentation time. Anonymous examiners will evaluate each poster and interaction for about 3 minutes.
5.  **The poster should be posted on the board from 14:00 to 17:30 on the day.** Check the presentation time through the session code.
