## 22ND F1TENTH AUTONOMOUS GRAND PRIX

F1TENTH Autonomous Racing is a competition organized by an international community of researchers, engineers, and autonomous systems enthusiasts. Teams participating in the 22nd F1TENTH Grand Prix at CDC 2024 will build a 1:10 scaled autonomous race car according to a given specification and write software for it to fulfill the competition objectives: Don’t crash and minimize laptime.

CDC 2024 will host an in-person competition, the 22nd F1tenth Autonomous Grand Prix, and a virtual benchmark competition, the 2nd F1tenth Sim Racing League. The main focus of the 22nd F1TENTH Autonomous Grand Prix is the in-person competition with physical cars. Each team will bring their own F1TENTH car and write the software for it. Teams have access to a detailed build manual and can use open-source software to get started. The organizers provide the race setup (rules, submissions, guidelines), the track, related infrastructure, and organize the race. For the competition, teams first participate in a qualification session for seeding. In this time trial, teams must demonstrate they can finish laps without colliding with track bounds. Afterwards, teams compete in a knockout phase in head-to-head racing. Since vehicles and hardware are standardized, teams must develop robust perception, planning, and control algorithms that can handle the uncertainties of a new track and new competitors. The Grand Prix phase pits competitors against each other on the same track.

The 2nd F1TENTH Sim Racing League is a virtual benchmark competition where teams can participate remotely. Teams will be provided with a simulator environment and a standardized car model. They will write software for their car to achieve the competition objectives: Don't crash and minimize laptime. Teams submit their software to the organizers, who will run it on the simulator and evaluate performance. Teams will be ranked based on their simulator performance. The Sim Racing League offers an opportunity to participate without building a physical car or traveling. For more information on the 2nd F1TENTH Sim Racing League, please visit the [2nd Sim Racing League website](https://autodrive-ecosystem.github.io/competitions/f1tenth-sim-racing-cdc-2024/).
