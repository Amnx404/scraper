# Oral Session Guideline

## How to present your paper?

*   **Time of Presentation:** 15 minutes. Presenters should ensure their presentation fits within the assigned time slot, leaving at least 3 minutes for questions. The official language is English.
*   Presentations will be given directly in the conference room.

## Presentation Materials

1.  Prepare your presentation file in Microsoft PowerPoint or Adobe PDF format in English. Bring a USB memory stick containing your file.
2.  It is recommended to save your material to the furnished notebook running Microsoft Windows before the session starts. Using your own laptop or smart device with an HDMI output is possible but not recommended.
3.  If your presentation includes animation or short movies, check technical aspects beforehand.

## Audio-Visual Equipment

1.  Each session room will have a Beam Projector and a Laptop. The provided computers will have Microsoft PowerPoint and Adobe Acrobat Reader. To avoid technical issues, presenters are strongly encouraged to use this equipment.
2.  Using your own laptop or smart device with an HDMI output is possible but not recommended. If unavoidable, bring necessary adaptors compatible with the beam projector and inform the session room staff in advance.
