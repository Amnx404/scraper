# Frequently Asked Questions (In-Person Competition)

## Mechanics

*   **Tires:** Any tires that fit the car's chassis are permitted, provided they do not damage the track or other cars. The organizers reserve the right to deem any tires unsuitable. For clarification or to request approval for alternative tires, inquire on [Slack](https://f1tenth-teams.slack.com/archives/C027T45B477).
    *   Currently allowed tires:
        *   [GRP Tyres T03 REVO](https://www.grpgandini.it/tyres/1-8-gt/t03-revo.html) (All compounds XB1 to XM7, all rim styles/colors)
        *   [GRP Tyres T04 SLICK](https://www.grpgandini.it/tyres/1-8-gt/t04-slick.html) (All compounds XB1 to XM8, all rim styles/colors)
        *   [Dynamite SpeedTreads Watch Dog (DYN8276)](https://nitrohobbies.com/dyn8276-2/)
        *   [AKA 1:8 Buggy Tires](https://www.raceaka.com/18-Buggy-Tires_c_7.html) (All sub-categories, compounds, inserts, and rims)
        *   [Contact RC tyres](https://www.racing-cars.com/wheels-tyres/on-road-tyres/brand-brand=contact-rc/) (Sh-types, all rims)
*   **Weight Limit:** There is no upper weight limit in the "restricted class."
*   **Added Weights:** Non-functional weights are only permitted if used to statically balance the car's wheels equally.
*   **Transmission Gear Ratio:** Changing the transmission gear ratio is not allowed. The competition emphasizes software development, and modifications beyond tire changes are discouraged to maintain focus on algorithms.

## Sensors

The following vehicle-mounted cameras are permitted in the "restricted class":

*   [Intel Realsense D455](https://www.intelrealsense.com/depth-camera-d455/): Yes
*   [Stereolabs ZED 2i](https://www.stereolabs.com/zed-2i/): Yes

The following are not permitted:

*   [Intel Realsense L515](https://www.intelrealsense.com/lidar-camera-l515/): No
*   [Intel Realsense T265](https://www.intelrealsense.com/tracking-camera-t265/): No
