## 22ND F1TENTH AUTONOMOUS GRAND PRIX

F1TENTH Autonomous Racing is a competition organized by an international community of researchers, engineers, and autonomous systems enthusiasts. Teams participating in the 22nd F1TENTH Grand Prix at CDC 2024 will build a 1:10 scaled autonomous race car according to a given specification and develop software to achieve the competition objectives: Don’t crash and minimize laptime.

CDC 2024 will host an in-person competition, the 22nd F1tenth Autonomous Grand Prix, and a virtual benchmark competition, the 2nd F1tenth Sim Racing League. The main focus of the 22nd F1TENTH Autonomous Grand Prix is the in-person competition with physical cars. Each team will bring their own F1TENTH car and write its software. Teams have access to a detailed build manual and can use open-source software to get started. Organizers provide the race setup (rules, submissions, guidelines), the track, related infrastructure, and manage the race.

For the competition, teams first participate in a qualification session for seeding. This session involves a time trial where teams must demonstrate they can finish laps without colliding with track boundaries. Afterward, teams compete in a knockout phase through head-to-head racing. Since vehicles and hardware are standardized, teams must develop robust perception, planning, and control algorithms capable of handling uncertainties of a new track and competitors. The Grand Prix phase pits competitors against each other on the same track.

The 2nd F1TENTH Sim Racing League is a virtual benchmark competition allowing remote participation. Teams will be provided with a simulator environment and a standardized car model. They will write software for their car to meet the competition objectives: Don't crash and minimize laptime. Submitted software will be run by organizers on the simulator for evaluation and ranking. The Sim Racing League offers an opportunity to participate without building a physical car or traveling. For more information, visit the [2nd Sim Racing League website](https://autodrive-ecosystem.github.io/competitions/f1tenth-sim-racing-cdc-2024/).
