We are providing the following information regarding inquiries from domestic authors about the BK21 standard for the ICCAS 2025 International Conference.

(We inform you that this is an international conference according to BK21 standards.)

**ICCAS 2025 Final Paper Submission Statistics**

*   Total submissions from 31 countries: 483 papers
    *   Oral presentation papers: 317 (175 domestic, 142 international)
*   Proportion of international papers among oral presentations: Approximately 44.79% (satisfies the requirement of over one-third)

**Standards for International Conferences Held in Korea**

1.  At least 4 participating countries
2.  At least 20 oral presentations
3.  More than one-third of oral presenters are foreign nationals
