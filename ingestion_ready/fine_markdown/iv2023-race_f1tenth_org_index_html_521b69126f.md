# F1Tenth IV2023 Championship

**Calender:** June 04 - June 07, 2023
**Location:** Anchorage, Alaska, USA

F1TENTH Autonomous Racing is a competition organized by an international community of researchers, engineers, and autonomous systems enthusiasts. Teams participating in the F1Tenth IV2023 Championship build a 1:10 scaled autonomous race car according to a given specification and write software to fulfill the competition objectives: Don’t crash and minimize laptime.

The F1TENTH IV2023 CHAMPIONSHIP focuses on an in-person competition with physical cars. Each team brings their own F1TENTH car and develops its software. Teams have access to a detailed build manual and can use open-source software to get started. The organizers provide the race setup (rules, submissions, guidelines), the track, and related infrastructure.

Competitions begin with a qualification round to determine seeding. Teams must demonstrate their ability to compete in a time trial on a map without obstacles and finish laps without collision on the same map with obstacles that are unknown prior to runtime. Afterward, teams compete in a knockout phase with head-to-head racing. Since vehicles and hardware are standardized, teams must develop robust perception, planning, and control algorithms that can handle the uncertainties of a new track and new competitors. The Championship phase pits competitors against each other on the same track.
