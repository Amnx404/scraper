# 25TH RoboRacer Autonomous Racing Competition

**2025 IEEE Conference on Decision and Control (CDC 2025)**
December 10th - 12th, 2025
Windsor Convention Center, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

## 25TH RoboRacer Autonomous Racing Competition

RoboRacer Autonomous Racing is a competition organized by an international community of researchers, engineers, and autonomous systems enthusiasts. Teams participating in the 25th RoboRacer Autonomous Racing Competition at CDC 2025 will build a 1:10 scaled autonomous race car according to a given specification and write software for it to achieve the competition objectives: Don’t crash and minimize laptime.

CDC 2025 will host an in-person competition with physical cars. Each team will bring their own RoboRacer car and develop its software. Teams have access to a detailed build manual and can utilize open-source software to get started. The organizers provide the race setup (rules, submissions, guidelines), the track, and related infrastructure.

The competition includes a qualification session for seeding, where teams compete in a time trial to demonstrate they can finish laps without colliding with track bounds. This is followed by a knockout phase with head-to-head racing. As vehicles and hardware are standardized, teams must develop robust perception, planning, and control algorithms to handle uncertainties of new tracks and competitors.
