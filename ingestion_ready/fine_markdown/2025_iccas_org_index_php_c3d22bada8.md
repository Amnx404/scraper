# 2025 The 4th F1Tenth Korea Championship

![ICCAS 2025 F1tneth Poster](http://2025.iccas.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/ICCAS-2025-F1tneth-Poster.png)

**2025 The 4th F1Tenth Korea Championship (now called Roboracer)**

The F1Tenth Competition (now renamed Roboracer) is an international event that brings together researchers, engineers, and enthusiasts in the field of autonomous systems. With active use in over 89 universities worldwide, Roboracer serves as a platform for cutting-edge research, education, and real-world testing of autonomous vehicle technologies.

The 2025 The 4th F1Tenth Korea Championship will take place as a fully **in-person, hardware-based competition**. Participating teams will design and build a **1:10 scale autonomous race car** following a provided reference specification. Each team will develop autonomous driving software that must fulfill three primary objectives: Don’t crash, minimize lap time, and overtake all opponents.

This competition focuses on challenging real-world racing scenarios involving multiple autonomous vehicles operating simultaneously. It is designed to access the current state-of-the-art in perception, planning, and control algorithms across a broad range of approaches—from classical model-based methods to modern learning-based systems.
