# The 2nd F1Tenth Korea Championship

The International Conference on Control, Automation, and Systems (ICCAS) 2023
Oct 17th - Oct 19th, 2023
Location: SonoCalm Yeosu at Yeosu, Korea

## F1TENTH Korea Championship

The F1TENTH autonomous driving competition is organized by an international community of researchers, engineers, and autonomous systems professionals.

The F1TENTH Korea Championship at ICCAS 2023 requires participating teams to build a 1:10 scale autonomous race car according to given specifications and write software to achieve the competition objectives.

ICCAS 2023 will host the competition as an in-person event.

**In-person Competition:** This format is for participants who will attend the competition in person in Yeosu, South Korea. Each team brings their physical F1TENTH car along with their software. The organizers provide the racing setup (rules, submissions, guidelines), track, and related infrastructure, and orchestrate the race itself.
