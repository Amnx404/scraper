# The 1st F1TENTH Korea Championship

**Date:** December 12th - December 13th, 2022
**Location:** Ramada Plaza by Wyndham Jeju, Jeju Island, Republic of Korea

The F1TENTH Autonomous Racing competition is organized by an international community of researchers, engineers, and autonomous systems enthusiasts. Participants build a 1:10 scaled autonomous race car and write software to "Don't crash and minimize laptime."

This event is an in-person competition where teams bring their own F1TENTH cars and software to Jeju Island. The organizers provide the race setup, track, infrastructure, and manage the race itself.
