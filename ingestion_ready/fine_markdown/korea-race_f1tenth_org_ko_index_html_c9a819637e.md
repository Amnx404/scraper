# The 1st F1TENTH Korea Championship

**Date:** December 12th - December 13th, 2022
**Location:** Ramada Plaza by Wyndham Jeju, Jeju Island, Republic of Korea

The F1TENTH Autonomous Driving Competition is organized by an international community of researchers, engineers, and autonomous systems engineers. Teams participating in the F1TENTH Korea Championship at KSMTE 2022 will build a 1:10 scale autonomous vehicle according to given specifications and write software to achieve the competition goals.

KSMTE 2022 will host the competition in a face-to-face format.

**Face-to-face competition:** This format is for participants attending the competition in person in Jeju Island, Korea. Each team brings their own F1TENTH car with their software. The organizers provide the race setup (rules, submissions, guidelines), track, and related infrastructure, and organize the race itself.
