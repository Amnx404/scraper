# Registration

This competition is open to everyone of all levels. Teams can consist of multiple teammates, or be a single person. Teams participating in the in-person competition must provide and build their own RoboRacer vehicle.

The Google form is for preliminary registration and for orientation and information sessions. Registration for IV 2024 is expected for all competitors.

[Registration Open](https://forms.gle/2JyDSVsBf25vDnWbA)
