## 10th F1TENTH Autonomous Grand Prix

F1TENTH Autonomous Racing is a competition organized by an international community of researchers, engineers, and autonomous systems enthusiasts. Teams participating in the 10th F1TENTH Grand Prix at ICRA 2022 will build a 1:10 scaled autonomous race car according to a given specification and write software for it to fulfill the competition objectives: Don’t crash and minimize laptime.

ICRA 2022 will host two variants of the competition:

1.  **In-person competition:** Participants travel to Philadelphia with their physical F1TENTH car and software. Organizers provide the race setup (rules, submissions, guidelines), track, infrastructure, and manage the race.
2.  **Virtual competition:** For participants unable to travel to Philadelphia. A simulation environment is used to run virtual race cars. Teams provide only the software; organizers provide the race setup (rules, submissions, guidelines), virtual track, virtual infrastructure (server), and manage the race.
