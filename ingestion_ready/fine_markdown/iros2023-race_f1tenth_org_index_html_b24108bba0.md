## 14th F1TENTH Autonomous Grand Prix

F1TENTH Autonomous Racing is a competition organized by an international community of researchers, engineers, and autonomous systems enthusiasts. Teams participating in the 14th F1TENTH Grand Prix at IROS 2023 will build a 1:10 scaled autonomous race car according to a given specification and develop software to achieve the competition objectives: "Don't crash and minimize laptime."

![](images/F1TENTH/f1tenth_video.gif)

### In-Person Competition at IROS 2023

The main focus of the 14th F1TENTH Autonomous Grand Prix is an in-person competition with physical cars. Each team will bring their own F1TENTH car and write the software for it. Teams have access to a detailed build manual and can utilize open-source software to facilitate their development. The organizers provide the race setup (rules, submissions, guidelines), the track, and related infrastructure.

The competition includes:
*   **Qualification:** Teams participate in a qualification round to determine seeding. They must demonstrate the ability to complete a time trial on a map without obstacles and finish laps without collision on the same map with unknown obstacles.
*   **Knockout Phase:** Teams compete in a knockout phase through head-to-head racing.

Since vehicles and hardware are standardized, teams must develop robust perception, planning, and control algorithms capable of handling the uncertainties of a new track and new competitors. The Grand Prix phase pits competitors against each other on the same track.
