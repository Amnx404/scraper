## Registration

This competition is open to everyone of all levels. Teams can consist of multiple teammates or be a single person. This is an in-person competition, and teams must bring their own F1TENTH cars.

Registration to BU 2024 is required for all competitors. Registration is **free** but is first-come-first-serve and will close when capacity is reached. Teams will need to arrange their own travel and accommodation.

[Registration Open](https://forms.gle/BsdLJAqXb1uzXzJFA)

### Participants

If you'd like to update any information about your team, you can register the modified information under the same team name, and we will update it accordingly.
