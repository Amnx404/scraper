## Registration

This competition is open to everyone of all levels. Teams can consist of multiple teammates or be a single person. Teams participating in the in-person competition must provide and build their own F1TENTH car.

### Google Site Registration

**For Race Participants:**
Sign up for race registration and more information by participating in an orientation and information session.
(Note: One of the team members must register with [ICCAS2023](https://2023.iccas.org/) to participate.)
