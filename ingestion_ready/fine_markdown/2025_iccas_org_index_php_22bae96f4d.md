# The 3rd Koh Young AI Competition 2025

**Date:** September 15 ~ October 10, 2025
**Website:** [http://KohYoungAi.iccas.org](http://KohYoungAi.iccas.org)
**Organized by:** Institute of Control Robotics and Systems (ICROS)
**Sponsored by:** Koh Young Technology

## Purpose

Recently, artificial intelligence (AI) has been widely applied to improve operational efficiency in various industrial fields. In line with this, ICROS and Koh Young ([https://kohyoung.com/kr](https://kohyoung.com/kr)), the world-leading SMT inspection solution provider, jointly host AI Challenge to encourage students to compete for an efficient decision-making problem in the manufacturing inspection process.

## Participants

Graduate and undergraduate students interested in AI development for industrial data are eligible to participate in the competition. The maximum number of members per team is limited to 5.

## Registration

1.  Download and fill out the registration form: [Click & Download the form](https://2025.iccas.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/3rd-Koh-Young-AI-Competition_Entry_Form.docx)
2.  Send the form to [conference@icros.org](mailto:conference@icros.org) and [sooheehan@postech.ac.kr](mailto:sooheehan@postech.ac.kr).

## Main Schedule

*   Registration opening: Aug. 11 – Sep. 12, 2025
*   Competition: Sep. 15 – Oct. 10, 2025
*   Award ceremony: Nov. 6, 2025

All winners must register for ICCAS 2025. Winners will also have the opportunity to present their algorithms in a special session and are required to open-source their solutions. The oral presentations of the 1st, 2nd and 3rd place winners will be recorded and uploaded to YouTube.

*Note: The competition schedule is subject to slight changes due to various circumstances.*

## Prizes

*   2nd place: 3,000,000 KRW
*   3rd place: 1,000,000 KRW
