## Registration

This competition is open to everyone of all levels. Teams can consist of multiple teammates or a single person. Teams participating in the in-person competition must provide and build their own F1TENTH car.

To register for the 2nd F1tenth Sim Racing League, please refer to the [Sim Racing Registration page](https://autodrive-ecosystem.github.io/competitions/f1tenth-sim-racing-cdc-2024/#registration).

The following Google form is for preliminary registration and for orientation and information sessions only. Registration for CDC 2024 is expected for all competitors.

[Registration Open](https://forms.gle/deacCAPfDxbLyTFa6)
