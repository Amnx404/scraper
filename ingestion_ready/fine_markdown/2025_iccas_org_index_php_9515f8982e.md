# Organized Session Proposal Submission

### How to Submit Your Paper

All Papers and organized session proposals must be submitted electronically through the [PaperCept Conference Management System](https://ras.papercept.net/conferences/scripts/start.pl).

Authors are advised to read:
*   [PaperCept’s Getting Started Manual for Authors](https://ras.papercept.net/conferences/support/support.php)
*   [PDF Compliance Guidelines](https://2024.iccas.org/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/Submission-Guideline.pdf)

[Organized Session Proposal Form](http://2025.iccas.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/Organized-Session-Proposal_ICCAS2025.docx)

> **[Go to PaperPlaza Conference Review Management System](https://ras.papercept.net/conferences/scripts/start.pl) and find ICCAS 2025.**
