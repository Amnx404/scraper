# Poster Session Guideline

## How to prepare your poster?

**For presenters:** Please prepare your poster in A0 (84 cm X 118 cm) in portrait format.

1.  The display panel size is approximately 1m wide and 2m high. Panels will be located in the lobby on the 3rd floor.
2.  Supplies for mounting (glue, tape, etc.) will be provided during the poster sessions.
3.  The recommended poster format is A0 (84 cm X 118 cm) portrait. Use an appropriate font size for readability. Posters must be printed beforehand and brought to the conference by the presenter.
4.  Authors should be present at their assigned poster panels to answer questions throughout the presentation time. Anonymous examiners will evaluate each poster and interaction for approximately 3 minutes.
5.  **Posters should be mounted on the board between 14:00 and 17:30 on the day of the session.** Verify your presentation time using the session code.
