This award is to recognize the outstanding papers first authored by a student based on the quality of the technical contribution and the presentation. All award winners will be announced at the Banquet.

**Eligibility:** The applicant should be a student and the first author. The student author also has to present the paper at the conference. The student is defined as a full-time student, enrolled for a degree or diploma at a university or other similar institution.

**Application:** Authors can apply for this award by choosing the Student Best Paper award application in the initial submission.

**Evaluation:** Award Committee members will review the candidate papers again after the regular review process. In addition, the Award Committee will examine the presentation during the conference. Please note that all the Best Student Paper Award candidates will be assigned to the oral session for presentation.
