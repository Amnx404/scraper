# Registration Guideline

## How to Register

1.  Login to [https://2025.iccas.org/](https://2025.iccas.org/). If you do not have an ID, please sign up.
2.  Navigate to My Page → Registration.

*   **Extra Paper Charge:** For presenters submitting more than one paper, there is an additional charge of USD 250 per paper. A registration kit is provided for one set per registration.

**Important Notes:**

*   All conference participants must complete registration.
*   Conference registration includes:
    *   Access to all sessions (excluding workshops).
    *   Downloadable E-proceeding.
    *   Welcome Reception.
    *   2 Lunch tickets.
    *   Banquet (included for regular registration; students may purchase a banquet ticket during registration).
*   **Member Rates:** Available for members of ICROS, IEEE IES, IEEE CSS, IEEE RAS, SICE, ISA, CAA, CAAI, ACA, CACS, and ECTI.
*   **Student Definition:** Full-time students enrolled in degree or diploma programs at a university or similar institution. Student status must be certified by the institution or advisor.
*   **Retiree Rate:** Applicable for individuals who are retired or not holding full-time employment at the time of registration.

**Registration Deadlines and Paper Submission:**

*   **Note 1:** One registration typically covers one paper. At least one author must complete an **advanced registration** by the final camera-ready paper submission deadline for the paper to be included in the conference program.
*   **Note 2:** A registration number is required to upload your final paper. Please complete registration before the final paper submission deadline.
*   **Note 3:** For presenters submitting multiple papers, an extra charge of USD 250 applies per additional paper.

## Payment

**Credit Card:** VISA, MasterCard, American Express, JCB, and Diners Club are accepted. (BC or Korean Exchange Cards are accepted only if issued in Korea.)

**Bank Transfer:**

1.  All bank charges are the responsibility of the participant.
2.  Complete the online registration form first. Transfer information will be provided via email upon form completion.
3.  **(For Korean participants):** For bank transfers (무통장 입금), you can request an electronic tax invoice (전자계산서). To do so, please email a copy of your business registration certificate along with your registration number (R\*\*\*\*) to [finance@icros.org](mailto:finance@icros.org). (Contact: Hae-ri Go, Manager, +82-2-6949-5801, ext. 0)

**Change Payment Method:** Send your full name and registration number (R\*\*\*\*) to [finance@icros.org](mailto:finance@icros.org). After payment cancellation, you can re-register via **My Page → Registration**.

## Cancellation Policy

Refunds of the registration fee will be issued after deducting a cancellation charge of USD 50, provided written notification is received before October 2, 2025. No refunds will be granted after October 3, 2025, or for no-shows.
