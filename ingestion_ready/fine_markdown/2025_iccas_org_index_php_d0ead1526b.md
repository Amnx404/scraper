# Tour

![attractions_combined.jpg](http://2025.iccas.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/attractions_combined.jpg)

## National Museum of World Writing Systems

Discover over 55 writing systems from ancient cuneiform to Hangul. This museum showcases the history and diversity of world scripts.
[Visit Official Website](https://mow.or.kr)

## National Hangeul Museum

Learn about the history and cultural significance of the Korean alphabet, Hangeul, through exhibitions and educational programs.
[Visit Official Website](https://www.hangeul.go.kr/en)

## Gaehang Port Area (Jung-gu, Incheon)

Explore Incheon’s open port history, Chinatown, and historical sites in the Gaehangjang district.
[VisitKorea Information](https://english.visitkorea.or.kr)

## Wolmido Island

Enjoy seaside attractions, amusement parks, cultural streets, and the beautiful coastal scenery of Wolmido.
[Incheon Jung-gu Official Page](https://www.icjg.go.kr)

## Jeondeungsa Temple (Ganghwa Island)

One of the oldest temples in Korea, founded in 381. Experience history and tranquility with optional temple stay programs.
[Official Website](https://jeondeungsa.org) | [Temple Stay Info](https://www.templestay.com/en)

## Ganghwa Peace Observatory

Observe North Korea through telescopes and learn about the Korean War and peace efforts at this unique observatory.
[VisitKorea Information](https://english.visitkorea.or.kr)

## Songdo Central Park Water Taxi

Take a scenic water taxi ride along the seawater canal in Songdo Central Park. Perfect for sightseeing and relaxing.
[Tourist Information](https://english.visitkorea.or.kr)
