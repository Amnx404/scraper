27th IEEE International Conference on Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITSC 2024)
September 24th - 27th, 2024
Edmonton Convention Centre, Edmonton, Canada

## 20TH F1TENTH AUTONOMOUS GRAND PRIX

F1TENTH Autonomous Racing is a competition organized by an international community of researchers, engineers, and autonomous systems enthusiasts. Teams participating in the 20th F1TENTH Grand Prix at ITSC 2024 will build a 1:10 scaled autonomous race car according to a given specification and write software for it to fulfill the competition objectives: Don’t crash and minimize laptime.

ITSC 2024 will host an In-person competition. The main focus is an in-person competition with physical cars. Each team will bring their own F1TENTH car and write the software for it. Teams have access to a detailed build manual and can use open-source software to get started. The organizers provide the race setup (rules, submissions, guidelines), the track, and related infrastructure, and organize the race.

For the competition, teams first take part in a qualification session to determine seeding. In this session, teams compete in a time trial, demonstrating their ability to finish laps without colliding with track bounds at run time. Afterwards, teams compete in a knockout phase in head-to-head racing. Since vehicles and hardware are standardized, teams must develop robust perception, planning, and control algorithms that can deal with the uncertainties of a new track and new competitors. The Grand Prix phase pits competitors against each other on the same track.
