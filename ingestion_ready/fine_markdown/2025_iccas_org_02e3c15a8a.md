# Registration Guideline

## How to Register

1.  Login to [https://2025.iccas.org/](https://2025.iccas.org/). If you do not have an ID, please sign up.
2.  Navigate to My Page → Registration.

*   For presenters submitting more than one paper, there is an extra paper charge of USD 250 per additional paper. A registration kit (one set per registration) will be provided.

**General Registration Information:**

*   All conference participants must complete registration.
*   Conference registration includes:
    *   Access to all sessions (excluding workshops)
    *   Downloadable E-proceeding
    *   Welcome Reception
    *   2 Lunch tickets
    *   Banquet (for regular registration; students may purchase banquet tickets during registration).
*   **Members** of ICROS, IEEE IES, IEEE CSS, IEEE RAS, SICE, ISA, CAA, CAAI, ACA, CACS, and ECTI are eligible for member registration fees.
*   **Students** are defined as full-time students enrolled in degree or diploma programs at a university or similar institution. Student status must be certified by the institution or advisor.
*   **Retiree:** Individuals who are retired or not holding full-time employment at the time of registration may register at the Retiree rate.

**Notes:**

1.  One registration generally covers one paper. At least one author must complete **advanced registration** by the final camera-ready paper submission deadline for their paper to be included in the conference program.
2.  A registration number is required to upload your final paper. Please complete registration before the final paper submission deadline.
3.  For presenters submitting multiple papers, an additional charge of USD 250 applies per extra paper.

## Payment

**Credit Card**: VISA, MasterCard, American Express, JCB, and Diners Club are accepted. (BC or Korean Exchange Cards are acceptable only if issued in Korea.)

**Bank Transfer**:

1.  All bank charges associated with the payment are the responsibility of the participant.
2.  Complete the online registration form first. Transfer information will be provided via email upon completion.
3.  (For Korean participants): You can request an electronic tax invoice for bank transfers (무통장 입금). To do so, please email a copy of your business registration certificate and your registration number (R\*\*\*\*) to [finance@icros.org](mailto:finance@icros.org). (Contact: Hae-ri Go, Manager, 02-6949-5801, ext. 0)

**Changing Payment Method**: Send your full name and registration number (R\*\*\*\*) to [finance@icros.org](mailto:finance@icros.org). After payment cancellation, you can re-register via **My Page → Registration**.

## Cancellation Policy

Refunds of the registration fee will be issued after deducting a cancellation charge of USD 50, provided written notification is received before October 2, 2025. No refunds will be granted after October 3, 2025, and refunds are not applicable for no-shows.
