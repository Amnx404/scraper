# Race 1

**Track:** Choose a closed-loop track. At Penn, students race around the hallways in the Levine building.

The hallways on the second floor of the Levine building form a rectangular track.

**Race Prep:** Teams should be given the track beforehand to practice.

**Race Day:** Refer to the [Race1 Rubric](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1gvkIyaZuPUC6-Cb7FpFiT8ZrpwOfUKHX/edit?usp=sharing&ouid=116660630717436430566&rtpof=true&sd=true) and [Grading](../grading.html#doc-grading) for evaluation criteria. If time permits, conduct a practice session. Otherwise, have teams run their vehicles around the track, recording lap times and the number of consecutive laps. Two heats allow teams two attempts to complete the race.
