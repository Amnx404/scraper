# Lecture 17 - Detection and Pose Estimation

**Overview:**

This lecture covers a vehicle tracking and prediction framework to detect the pose of an obstacle vehicle and predict its probable future poses, addressing the challenge of accounting for other moving vehicles in a race or driving scenario.

**Topics Covered:**

*   Single view geometry
*   Pose estimation
*   Homography
*   Trajectory prediction
*   Using April tags
