### ICCAS with IJCAS option

The International Conference on Control, Automation, and Systems (ICCAS) aims to bring together researchers and engineers in the fields of control, automation, robotics, and systems engineering. The scope of the conference is well aligned with the scope of the International Journal of Control, Automation, and Systems (IJCAS). The ICCAS with IJCAS option provides a possibility to publish a paper in the Journal while presenting it at ICCAS.

*   Authors can submit an extended abstract (1 or 2 pages) to ICCAS and simultaneously submit a journal version (at least 6 pages) to IJCAS.
*   The submission deadline for joint contributions is May 31, 2025.
*   Authors of conference papers submitted to ICCAS 2025 ([https://2025.iccas.org](https://2025.iccas.org)) are encouraged to submit extended versions (at least 6 pages) to the Special Issue of ICCAS 2025 of the International Journal of Control, Automation, and Systems (IJCAS).
*   Manuscripts submitted to the special issue should be an extended version of the conference paper and must not have been published or be under review elsewhere. They will undergo peer-review following the IJCAS author guidelines.
*   Accepted papers are tentatively scheduled for publication in the 2025 issue of IJCAS.

**What is the ICCAS with IJCAS option?**
A manuscript submitted to ICCAS with the IJCAS option may be accepted by both ICCAS and IJCAS, by one of them, or by neither.
*   If accepted only by IJCAS, it will appear on Web of Science as a journal paper.
*   If accepted only by ICCAS, it will be considered a normal ICCAS paper.

**How to submit papers to ICCAS and IJCAS?**
1.  **At ICCAS** ([https://2025.iccas.org](https://2025.iccas.org)): Select the "Yes" option: "I like to submit the extended version of this paper to IJCAS."
    *   If you select "No" and IJCAS accepts your extended paper, the conference paper will not be indexed in IEEE Xplore. You must also present the paper at ICCAS 2025.
2.  **At IJCAS** ([https://ijcas.org](https://ijcas.org)): Select the "Special Issue: ICCAS 2025" category.
3.  After submitting to both ICCAS and IJCAS, inform the IJCAS Secretariat at [journal@ijcas.com](mailto:journal@ijcas.com) of your ICCAS paper number.
    *   The corresponding author needs to review at least 2 papers in IJCAS.

**How to submit the final papers to ICCAS?**
When submitting a paper under the ICCAS + IJCAS option, upload the ICCAS final paper (for short papers, a 2-3 page extended abstract) by the ICCAS deadline (August 31).
*   If your IJCAS manuscript is ultimately accepted, the finalized ICCAS paper will appear only in the ICCAS proceedings and will not be posted on IEEE Xplore, regardless of your initial selection.
*   If the IJCAS manuscript is rejected, the finalized ICCAS paper will be published in the ICCAS proceedings, and inclusion in IEEE Xplore will depend on whether you chose to have your ICCAS paper posted there.

**Important Dates for IJCAS:**
*   **Submission of Manuscripts:** May 31, 2025 (Korean Standard Time, UTC +9 hours). No extension for ICCAS with IJCAS Option.
*   **Notification of Acceptance:** September 30, 2025 (Extended from August 31, 2025)
*   **Submission of Final Papers:** October 31, 2025
*   **Publication:** January 2026 (Tentative)

### ICCAS 2025 will be held on November 4(Tue)-7(Fri), 2025

The 25th International Conference on Control, Automation, and Systems (ICCAS 2025) will be held from November 4-7, 2025, at Songdo ConvensiA in Incheon, Korea. ICCAS is an annual international conference hosted for the last 25 years by the Institute of Control, Robotics, and Systems. It aims to provide a forum for researchers and industry practitioners to share original research results and practical development experiences on new challenges and emerging issues in control, automation, robotics, and systems.

Accepted papers will be published in the ICCAS 2025 Proceedings. Papers of 3-6 pages will be indexed in IEEE Xplore, SCOPUS, and EI Compendex, and will be submitted for indexing in Clarivate Analytics Conference Proceedings Citation Index (Web of Science).
