## 19TH F1TENTH AUTONOMOUS GRAND PRIX

F1TENTH Autonomous Racing is a competition organized by an international community of researchers, engineers, and autonomous systems enthusiasts. Teams participating in the 19th F1TENTH Grand Prix at SM 2024 will build a 1:10 scaled autonomous race car according to a given specification and write software for it to achieve the competition objectives: Don’t crash and minimize laptime.

SM 2024 will host an in-person competition with physical cars. Each team will bring their own F1TENTH car and write the software for it. Teams have access to a detailed build manual and can use open-source software. The organizers provide the race setup (rules, submissions, guidelines), the track, and related infrastructure.

For the competition, teams first participate in a qualification session for seeding. This session involves a time trial where teams must demonstrate they can finish laps without colliding with track bounds or randomly placed obstacles. Afterwards, teams compete in a knockout phase through head-to-head racing. Since vehicles and hardware are standardized, teams must develop robust perception, planning, and control algorithms to handle uncertainties of a new track and new competitors. The Grand Prix phase pits competitors against each other on the same track.
