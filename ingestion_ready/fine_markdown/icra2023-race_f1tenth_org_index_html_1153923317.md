# 11th F1TENTH Autonomous Grand Prix

**Conference:** IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA) 2023
**Dates:** May 29th - June 1st, 2023
**Location:** Excel London, UK

[F1TENTH ICRA 2023](index.html)

## 11th F1TENTH Autonomous Grand Prix

F1TENTH Autonomous Racing is a competition organized by an international community of researchers, engineers, and autonomous systems enthusiasts. Teams participating in the 11th F1TENTH Grand Prix at ICRA 2023 will build a 1:10 scaled autonomous race car according to a given specification and develop software to achieve the competition objectives: "Don't crash and minimize laptime."

![](images/F1TENTH/f1tenth_video.gif)

### In-Person Competition at ICRA 2023

The 11th F1TENTH Autonomous Grand Prix at ICRA 2023 will feature an in-person competition with physical cars. Each team will bring their own F1TENTH car and develop its software. Teams will have access to a detailed build manual and can utilize open-source software to facilitate their development. The organizers will provide the race setup (rules, submissions, guidelines), the track, and related infrastructure, and will manage the race itself.

**Competition Phases:**

1.  **Qualification:** Teams will participate in a qualification round to determine seeding. They must demonstrate their ability to compete in a time trial on a map without obstacles and complete laps without collision on the same map with obstacles that are unknown prior to runtime.
2.  **Knockout Phase:** Following qualification, teams will compete in a knockout phase, racing head-to-head. This phase emphasizes the performance of their autonomous driving algorithms.

Since vehicles and hardware are standardized, teams must develop robust perception, planning, and control algorithms capable of handling the uncertainties of a new track and new competitors. The Grand Prix phase will pit competitors against each other on the same track.
