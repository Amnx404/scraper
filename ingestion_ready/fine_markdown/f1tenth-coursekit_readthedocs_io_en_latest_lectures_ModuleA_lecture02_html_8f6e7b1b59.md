# Lecture 2 - Automatic Emergency Braking

**Overview:**

This lecture covers the importance of safety when working with autonomous systems. When is a system “safe enough” to deploy? You will learn about how Automatic Emergency Braking (AEB) works in today’s vehicles, as well as methods for gathering information about the outside world so the vehicle can respond appropriately. Once the vehicle has acquired real-world data and reacts to its situation accordingly, how do we measure how “safe” the reaction is?

**Topics Covered:**

*   Automatic Emergency Braking (AEB)
*   Range Sensors for Autonomous Vehicles
*   Working with Laser Scan Data
*   Notions of Correctness
